Travel

Drupalcamp Colorado 2011 will be in downtown Denver on the Auraria Campus.

Event Location

King Center of the Auraria Higher Education Center
855 Lawrence Way, Denver, CO.

  • Friday night's party is at Casselman's
  • Saturday night's party is at Brooklyn's

View Drupalcamp Colorado 2011 in a larger map

AHEC Campus and parking map: http://www.ahec.edu/parking/ParkingMap.pdf

Venue map

The conference takes place in the King Center. Rooms in use are the Concert Hall, Recital Hall, and several classrooms. View the venue map

Light rail, bus, etc.

The location is convenient for many modes of mass transit with both bus and light rail stations within a few blocks. You can use the above Google map to get directions from your home, then select "bus" mode to see options and schedule. RTD Denver has more details on public transport.

Biking

Ride your bike! The platte and Cherry Creek trails will both get you right near the campus from miles around.

Parking

Recommended visitor parking is in the Tivoli Lot at Ninth St and Auraria Parkway located directly west of the Tivoli Student Union or the Parking and Transportation Centre. Use PDF map to see where the parking structure relates to the King Center where the conference is held.

Lodging

Being so close to downtown Denver there are many nearby hotels.
