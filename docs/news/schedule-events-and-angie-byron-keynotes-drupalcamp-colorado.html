Schedule of events and Angie Byron keynotes at Drupalcamp Colorado

Posted by Ben on June 1st, 2011

The schedule of events at Drupalcamp Colorado has been posted, including pre-camp paid workshops, evening parties and Angie "webchick" Byron providing the keynote presentation on getting involved in the Drupal community.

Known as webchick on drupal.org and elsewhere online, Angie Byron is a long-time and passionate open-source contributor, Drupal 7 co-maintainer, Drupal 8 bug hunter, and co-author of the O'Reilly book Using Drupal. We are excited to have Angie's presence and enthusiasm in Denver this June 11th and 12th! True fact, Angie Byron can beat Super Mario Bros in 7 minutes and 3 seconds.

Along with one of the core maintainers for the Drupal codebase, Drupalcamp will have 25 total Drupal 7 core contributors including some amazingly prolific ones who combined were responsible for approximately 13% of the code changes in Drupal 7. And along with that great Drupal-specific knowledge will be awesome representation from national and local businesses, including our many company sponsors. We're expecting close to 400 attendees in total at the conference, making it likely this will be the Drupalcamp with the highest attendance in North America.

  • Register now if you haven't, tickets increase to $50 on June 6th
  • Pre-camp workshops sold separately and seats are going fast!

You can also read a bit more on what to expect at the conference.

See you June 11th and 12th!
