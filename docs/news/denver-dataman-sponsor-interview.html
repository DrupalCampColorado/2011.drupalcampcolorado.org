Denver DataMan - sponsor interview

Posted by juaneclark on June 3rd, 2011

What does your company do and how do you work with Drupal?
Denver DataMan is a training and software development company that works with small to medium sized businesses and nonprofits to produce tools that help them to be more efficient and effective in their job. Our training driven solutions mean our clients understand the process and have the skills to use their tools well.

Our primary tool in web development and much of our application development is Drupal.

We also work with Drupal in terms of usability testing, recommendations and site reviews. We can come
in as Denver DataMan or we can work under the flag of other development firms with these services.

How did you first get involved in Drupal?
Denver DataMan got involved with Drupal because our clients asked us to start building tools and not just training on the tools they had. Drupal gives us a platform from which to build robust systems as well as an easy method for clients to add, remove, and overall manage their content.

What type of projects do you do outside of Drupal?
Denver DataMan specializes in Microsoft Office, contact relationship management (CRM), and content management systems (CMS) training. We create custom tailored training material to focus only on what the client needs. Our trainings are supplemented with training manuals that incorporate images and easy to understand text.

Is there anything else you'd like our attendees to know?
Denver DataMan is giving away a free Strategy Report on Three Strategies for Integrating Drupal in Small Business and you can go to the Denver DataMan website (http://www.denverdataman.com/forms/pre-drupal-camp-colorado-strategy-rep...) to pre-register to get the report when it is released at Drupal Camp Colorado.

See Denver DataMan's sponsor profile: http://2011.drupalcampcolorado.org/sponsors/denver-dataman
