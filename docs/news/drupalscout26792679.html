WebEnabled - sponsor interview

Posted by juaneclark on June 3rd, 2011

What does your company do and how do you work with Drupal?
We provide a development, staging, and production hosting platform for Drupal and other open source applications like Wordpress, Joomla, and Magento.

With WebEnabled (WE), a Drupal site can be automatically setup for you under a minute. The site automatically gets a webenabled.net URL and comes pre-installed with Drush, phpMyAdmin, SVN, and Git. You also get full SSH and SCP/SFTP access.
