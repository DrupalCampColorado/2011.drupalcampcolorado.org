Call for trainers at DrupalCamp Colorado 2011

Posted by Ben on April 6th, 2011

We would like to offer pre-camp training on the day before DrupalCamp Colorado 2011 this summer. If you and your organization provides Drupal training and would like to do so for DrupalCamp Colorado please fill out this webform http://2011.drupalcampcolorado.org/content/call-trainers

Once we have secured one or more training providers we'll provide signup here on this site.

Update: Call for trainers will close April 13th, so please submit your interest before then.

Update 2: The form is now closed. trainers will be select and announced on the week of April 18th.

June 11th and 12th at Denver's King Center - Dates and Venue Announced!

Posted by greggles on February 16th, 2011

Drupalcamp Colorado 2011 will be June 11th and 12th at the King Center.

The King Center is the major performing arts facility on the Auraria Campus (Auraria is a combination of CU Denver/Metro State/Community College of Denver).

The event has a capacity of 500 people and there seems like a good chance we will fill the entire space.

For more information you can subscribe to the news rss feed or @DrupalColorado on twitter.
