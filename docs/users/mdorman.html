Bio

Matthew Dorman is a Project Manager at NorthPoint Solutions managing numerous projects utilizing Drupal and other Web Content Management Systems. He has background building large content sites such as People.com, EW.com, Parade.com, MTV.com Blogs, CarAndDriver.com, Menshealth.com, Womenshealthmag.com and more.

He also maintains his own Drupal powered site (Tag Sell It) which aggregates over 50,000 events each week for display on the site and 4 iOS Apps.
