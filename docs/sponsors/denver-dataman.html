Denver DataMan

Denver DataMan is a computer training and web application development company. We specialize in helping small businesses leverage technology tools through comprehensive training programs and the customization of their business-necessary tools. Years of experience within the Drupal community prepares us to work with end-users as well as developers looking to advance their skill sets.

Our training places the specific knowledge and information needed by our clients into their hands, providing custom site manuals and in-person training. We are a full service development firm, however, we can work with developers and other firms to design end-user training and usability testing.

The foundation of our success is based upon a rigorous research and planning phase. No software is implemented without first knowing our clients needs and goals, and having a workable strategy to put it in place.

More than just a training company, Denver DataMan also provides websites, CRM implementations, custom software solutions, integration between systems, and social marketing strategies.

Contact us from our website, like us on Facebook, follow us on Twitter, or fill out this form to get our 2011 Strategy Report on Three Tools for Integrating Drupal in Small Business.

Denver DataMan wants to be your training superhero. We can help you get the right information to the right people at the right time.
