AppliedTrust Seeking a Lead Drupal Architect

Posted by paul on June 13th, 2011

AppliedTrust is looking for a talented, motivated Drupal whiz with several years of Drupal experience and a proven history of community contribution to play a lead role in our Drupal practice, with emphasis on eCommerce and Enterprise Applications, and increasing visibility for AppliedTrust in these areas.

What are just a few things that we can offer you?

  • Challenge, Fun and the ability to create and innovate. This position would be a great fit for someone excited about showing how Drupal can come in and kick the butts of other hundred thousand or million dollar apps.
  • A Fantastic Work Environment. Just ask us about Bacon Day, the 2011 Grill Master Series, First Things First, the Mule Tape Festival, the Social Chair, the Kegerator, K-Cups & Zuberfizz...and many more!
  • Opportunity. Shape your position and your company! In a small company of peers, your voice stands out and you can most definitely make a difference, from your personal career path all the way to what we do as a company.

For more details and to apply, please check out http://www.appliedtrust.com/jobs/lead-drupal-architect

Please feel free to reach out to me directly with questions: paul@atrust.com
