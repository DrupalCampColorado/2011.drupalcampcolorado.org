Commerce Guys hiring Drupal experts

Posted by rszrama on June 12th, 2011

Commerce Guys is hiring Drupal experts in the US and Europe to help make Drupal the number one open source e-commerce platform in the world. We develop and consult on the largest e-commerce sites being developed today with Drupal 7, and we promote the development and use of Drupal Commerce through code sprints, Drupal events, and e-commerce industry events. As we grow the Commerce ecosystem and bring more professional service providers into our partner network, we're always on the lookout for Drupal experts to help us.

If you have a passion for Drupal and experience with e-commerce, we'd love to hear from you. We're looking for developers to help us build Commerce solutions and e-commerce site architects to consult with our partners on their major e-commerce projects. Working with Commerce Guys will provide opportunities to learn, to work with some of the top contributors in the community, and to travel to promote the company and Commerce technology.

If you work out of Paris, you might even get free cooking lessons. (Jealous...)

Stop by our table near the Recital Hall or get in touch via our contact form to learn more.
